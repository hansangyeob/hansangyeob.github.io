Intro

My pleasure to meet you all. My name is Han sang yoeb. You can call me Tony. My student number is S3821179 and the email is S3821179@rmit.edu.vn. I am from south Korea and i can speak Korean, english and little bit of Vietnamese!

I have worked as a tour guide in Vietnam for a one year from 2017 to 2018. I am really interested in Vietnam as the country is experiencing booming economy. As a student, I thought that I could learn more from it compare to the other developed countries.

Work

There are many possible career paths that IT influences. These days, machine learning (so called deep learning), cloud computing, blockchain technology gets the limelight from all over the world. However, my personally interest is in IoT and gaming industry. When I was in university in Korea, my major was Mechanical engineering design, which is subject mostly dealing with the hardware. While I was learning about designing hardware, I had chance to learn about ‘Arduino’. It is so simple mechanism, electricity enables the hardware to move but software is crucial in this part. This simple mechanism has really got my attention, which was making something with the sensors as the way I demand. This little event made me curious about IT.

After dropped out of university in Korea, I came Vietnam to explore new culture whether it is suitable for my future career or not. When I was searching for suitable international school in HCM, I have found out that one of my Vietnamese friends have already graduated RMIT and working for Samsung as an engineer. I wasn’t really sure back at the time, so I have decided to enroll language course in RMIT. Fortunately, experience in language courses helped me to made decision to enroll IT as my major.

When I was in university in Korea, I have learned to not give so much expectation which school will provide me. Being an active student will extend my chance to find a job.

About

https://careers.blizzard.com/en-us/openings/information-technology/all/all/all/1

Blizzard entertainment is one of the famous gaming companies in the world. As the gaming industry is gaining many customers and keep developing complicated software, the demand for steady server operation is crucial. Cloud systems engineer is necessary in Blizzard entertainment due to providing a world class hybrid cloud with global reach. They focus on technologies that makes their company more efficient, consistent, agile and portable across private and public cloud. Majority of experts is saying that cloud computing technology will be one of leading technology in the future. Personally, it was really appealing to be due to the idea that hardware is not really important when people have enough cloud technology. Many companies waste tones of industrial waste during process of making household electrical appliances. I thought that people could save tones of materials if we develop cloud computing. To be able to work as a cloud systems engineer, various of computing skills, knowledge and experience are required. Specially in Blizzard entertainment, they run one of the largest private Openstack clouds in the world across seven geographical sites. For this reason, able to work in blizzard as a cloud systems engineer, I need to become familiar with toe core Openstack products, intermediate Linux experience, Python and lastly, infrastructure as Code concepts and related tools like Git, Terraform, Ansible and Puppet. For me, Python and Git is working on the most these days. To gain more knowledge about software, I am trying to find small team project which I could design my own code. I want to try more practical things with coding.
